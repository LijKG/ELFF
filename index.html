ELFF

We are a social enterprise that aims to promote ethical and sustainable living in Ethiopia and beyond.

Our Services

We offer a range of services to help you live more ethically and sustainably.

Eco-friendly Products

We sell eco-friendly products that are made from natural, organic, and recycled materials. Our products include clothing, accessories, home decor, and more.

Educational Workshops

We organize educational workshops on topics such as environmental awareness, waste management, renewable energy, and more. Our workshops are interactive, fun, and informative.

Empowerment Programs

We support empowerment programs that aim to improve the lives of marginalized communities. Our programs include skill development, microfinance, social entrepreneurship, and more.
